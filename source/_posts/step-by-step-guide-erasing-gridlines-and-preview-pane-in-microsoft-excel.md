 Have you ever been working on an Excel spreadsheet when dotted lines suddenly appeared? While fine to some, those dotted lines may be distracting to others. We'll show you when and why these dotted lines appear and how to remove them.

 There are a few instances where you may see dotted lines in Excel. If you set up your sheet to print or use the Page Break Preview, you'll see dotted lines for page breaks when you return to the Normal view. Additionally, if you [share a spreadsheet with editing privileges](https://vimeo-videos.techidaily.com/innovating-content-creation-vimeo-edition/), a collaborator may add a border with a dotted line style.

 Here's how to remove dotted lines in Excel for these situations.

##  Remove Dotted Lines After a Printer Preview

 If you print your sheet or simply [look at a print preview](https://some-guidance.techidaily.com/updated-tuning-into-emotion-background-melodies-in-film/) of it, you'll notice those dotted lines appear when you return to the sheet. These are indicators of page breaks for your spreadsheet.

![Dotted lines after seeing the print preview](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/PrinterLines-ExcelRemoveDottedLines.png) 

Related: [How to Print a Specific Selection of Cells in Excel](https://some-guidance.techidaily.com/updated-tuning-into-emotion-background-melodies-in-film/) 

 To remove the dotted lines, go to File > Options.

 When the Excel Options window opens, select "Advanced" on the left. On the right, scroll down to the Display Options for This Worksheet section. Uncheck the box for Show Page Breaks and click "OK."

![Show Page Breaks unchecked](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/UncheckShowBreaks-ExcelRemoveDottedLines.png) 

 When the Options window closes, you should see the dotted lines removed from your spreadsheet.

![Dotted lines removed after seeing the print preview](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/PrinterNoLines-ExcelRemoveDottedLines.png) 

 Follow the same steps for additional sheets in your workbook.

As with printer previews, you'll see lines for the page breaks.

![Page Break Preview dotted and solid lines](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/PageBreakLines-ExcelRemoveDottedLines.png)
